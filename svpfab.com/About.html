SVP Fab & Electro Engineering

SVP Fab & Electro Engineering Pvt Ltd. is engaged in manufacturing of custom made Heavy Steel Fabrication & Epoxy Moulded Components. We started our business operation in the field of Heavy Steel Fabrication in 2007 and over the years it has grown from strength to strength and has earned a reputation to be proud of.

The company is located in the central part of India - Mandideep, a well known Industrial hub of country in the state of Madhya Pradesh.

Our company is a registered SSI unit, which caters to the need of the industry and is now managed by two brothers (Mr. Shankar Bhojwani and Mr. Vashudev Bhojwani). No product leaves the factory without undergoing quality checks carefully formulated quality plans.

We use premium quality material in manufacturing our products which has enabled us to carve a niche in this domain. Our industry experience has also helped us to understand keep pace with the prevailing and emerging demands in the competitive markets. Being an ambitious company, we develop the products at our state of art infrastructure unit, which is equipped with modern machinery, shot blasting and testing of manufacturing goods.

Owing of our professional approach, we are able to cater a wide range base spread across the country. Our esteemed clients are:-
• Andritz Hydro India Pvt Ltd
• BHEL Bhopal
• Crompton Greaves (Traction Division)
• Crompton Greaves (Generator Plant)
• Jeumont Electric India Pvt Ltd
• Marathon Electric Motor (India) Ltd
• Voith Hydro Pvt Ltd
